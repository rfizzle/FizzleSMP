## Infinite Trading
- **CurseForge ID:** 348964
- **Slug:** infinite-trading
- **Modrinth Slug:** infinite-trading
- **Mod Loader:** Fabric
- **Side:** server
- **Summary:** Prevents villager trades from locking up, allowing infinite trading with villagers and wandering traders.
- **Why:** Removes the frustration of trade lockouts on the SMP, letting players buy and sell freely without waiting for restocks.
- **Dependencies:** Collective
- **Conflicts:** None known
