# Exploration & World Guide

FizzleSMP completely overhauls world generation across all dimensions and adds two new dimensions. This guide covers what's new and how to navigate it all.

---

## Fast Travel — Waystones

Waystones are the main teleportation system on the server.

### How to Use
1. **Find a Waystone** — they generate naturally in villages. You can also craft one.
2. **Activate it** — right-click the waystone to add it to your network.
3. **Name it** — give it a memorable name so you can find it later.
4. **Teleport** — right-click any activated waystone to see all waystones you've discovered and teleport between them.

### Other Teleport Items
- **Warp Scroll** — single-use item that teleports you to any known waystone.
- **Warp Stone** — rechargeable item for teleporting without being at a waystone.
- Waystones can be set to **global** so all players can see them (useful for community hubs).

---

## Navigation Tools

- **Explorer's Compass** — craft it to locate specific structures. Right-click to open the search UI, select a structure type, and the compass points the way.
- **Nature's Compass** — same concept but for biomes. Craft it and search for any biome.
- **Xaero's Minimap** — shows a minimap on your HUD. Press **Y** to open waypoint manager. Death points are marked automatically.
- **Xaero's World Map** — press **M** to open a full-screen world map showing explored terrain.

## Overworld Changes

### Terrain — Terralith + Tectonic + Geophilic

The overworld terrain is dramatically different from vanilla:

- **Terralith** adds 100+ new biomes with unique terrain shapes, trees, and vegetation.
- **Tectonic** reshapes terrain generation for more dramatic mountains, valleys, and coastlines.
- **Geophilic** adds small nature details — fallen logs, rocks, leaf piles, bushes.
- **Terraphilic** (datapack) blends Terralith biomes with Geophilic details.

### Loot

**Better Loot** and **Loot Integrations** enrich the loot tables of all these structures. Chests have better, more varied rewards. **Lootr** makes all loot chests per-player — everyone gets their own loot from the same chest.

### Structure Density

**Sparse Structures** is installed to prevent structure oversaturation. Structures generate at reasonable distances from each other.

## New Dimensions

### The Bumblezone (Bee Dimension)

A bee-themed dimension full of honeycomb, bees, unique mobs, and structures.

**How to enter:** Throw an **Ender Pearl at a Bee Nest** (or Beehive). You'll be teleported in.

**How to leave:** Various methods are available inside the dimension (look for exit structures or use waystones).

**What's inside:**
- Unique biomes made of honeycomb and honey
- New mobs and bosses
- Bee-related items, blocks, and progression
- Structures to explore

**Warning:** Taking honey without permission makes the bees VERY angry.

### The Otherside (Deeper and Darker)

A sculk-themed dimension beyond the Deep Dark, accessed through Ancient Cities.

**How to enter:** Explore **Ancient Cities** in the Deep Dark (y-level -51). Look for the portal structure within the city.

**What's inside:**
- Sculk-themed biomes
- New blocks, armor, and materials
- The Warden's domain — endgame content
- Unique mobs and challenges

This gives Ancient Cities a real purpose beyond just finding the Ward music disc.

## New Creatures

Several mods add new mobs you'll encounter while exploring:

- **Creeper Overhaul** — Biome-specific creeper variants (desert, jungle, ocean, etc.) with custom models. They behave like normal creepers but look different per biome.
- **Creeper Healing** — After a creeper explodes, the terrain **regenerates over time**. No more permanent creeper craters.
- **Friends & Foes** — Adds mobs from rejected Minecraft mob votes: Copper Golems, Glare, Moobloom, Iceologer, and more. Found in various biomes.
- **Critters and Companions** — Ambient wildlife you can find and tame. Adds atmosphere to exploration.
- **Villager Names** — Villagers now have randomly generated names displayed above their heads.

## Tips

- Use **Explorer's Compass** to find specific structures when you're looking for something.
- Place **Waystones** at interesting locations for easy return trips.
- Structure loot is per-player (Lootr), so exploring with friends doesn't mean competing for loot.
- **Magnum Torch** prevents mob spawning in a large radius — place one at your base to keep it safe.
