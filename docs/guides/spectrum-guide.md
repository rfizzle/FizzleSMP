# Spectrum Guide

Spectrum is a large, progression-based mod about harnessing the power of color. It has its own discovery system, crafting mechanics, and advancement tree. This guide covers how to get started — for full details, use the in-game guidebook.

---

## Getting Started

### The Guidebook

Spectrum uses **Modonomicon** for its in-game guidebook. This is your primary reference for Spectrum content.

1. **Craft the guidebook** — check EMI for the recipe (it's an early-game craft).
2. **Read it** — the book guides you through Spectrum's progression step by step.
3. **Follow the advancements** — Spectrum gates content behind discoveries. You unlock new pages in the guidebook as you progress.

### Discovery System — Revelationary

Spectrum uses a discovery system where new blocks and items are **hidden until you unlock them**. You won't see Spectrum content in EMI until you've made the relevant advancement. This is intentional — the mod is designed around gradual discovery.

**Don't panic if you can't find Spectrum recipes in EMI.** Progress through the guidebook to unlock them.

---

## Core Progression

### Phase 1: Color and Pigments

Your first steps involve:
- Gathering colored items (dyes, flowers, etc.)
- Learning to extract pigments
- Crafting your first Spectrum workstations

### Phase 2: Pedestal Crafting

The **Pedestal** is Spectrum's main crafting station. Unlike normal crafting:
- Items are placed on the pedestal and surrounding blocks
- Crafting requires specific colored gemstones as fuel
- Recipes unlock as you progress through the guidebook

### Phase 3: Advanced Content

Later progression includes:
- Unique enchantments and tools
- New blocks with special properties
- Advanced crafting stations
- Color-based magic mechanics

---

## Key Mechanics

### Colored Gemstones

Spectrum introduces colored gemstone types that serve as crafting fuel and progression materials. You'll mine these underground and process them through Spectrum's crafting chains.

### Pedestal Network

Advanced Spectrum crafting involves placing items on pedestals and surrounding blocks in specific patterns. The guidebook shows exact layouts for each recipe.

### Pastel Network

A late-game energy/transport system unique to Spectrum.

---

## Tips

- **Always check the guidebook first.** Spectrum is designed to be self-documenting through its advancement-gated book.
- **Follow the advancement tree** — don't try to skip ahead. Content unlocks in order.
- **EMI will show recipes as you unlock them.** If a recipe is missing, you haven't reached that point yet.
- **Spectrum has its own tools and weapons** that work with Better Combat animations.
- Spectrum has known interactions with **Tech Reborn** and **Mythic Upgrades** — these are handled by fix datapacks already included in the modpack.

---

## Integration with Other Mods

- **Better Combat** — Spectrum weapons have proper attack animations.
- **Tech Reborn** — Cross-mod recipe conflicts resolved via the `Spectrum-TechReborn-Recipe-Fix.zip` datapack.
- **Mythic Upgrades** — Integration errors fixed via the `Spectrum-MythicUpgrades-Fix.zip` datapack.
- **BeyondEnchant** — Level cap conflicts resolved via the `Spectrum-BeyondEnchant-LevelCap-Fix.zip` datapack.
